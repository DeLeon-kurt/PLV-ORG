ACES

PLV Association of Civil Engineering Students is another popular student organization within Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Valenzuela. This student group is officially considered as the governing body of all students studying in the Civil Engineering course within PLV. The said organization would provide a wide array of activities and support aimed at professional and academic development of the members.
