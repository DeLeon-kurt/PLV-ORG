About Us

Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Valenzuela Organization Directory (PLV ORG) is an online directory website for the student organizations under the Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Valenzuela. PLV ORG contains active academic and non-academic organizations and how they perform inside the campus. The website will serve as a guide on what the organizations are for and what they have been doing throughout the years for the wellness of their fellow students. The developers of PLV ORG show their gratitude to everyone in the acknowledgments.
